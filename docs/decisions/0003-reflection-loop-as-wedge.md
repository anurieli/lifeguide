# 0003 — The closed reflection loop is the product wedge

**Status:** Accepted · 2026-05-20 · (full feature is post-v1; the decision shapes v1 data)

## Context
Of ~18 products studied (Tana, Mem, Reflect, Day One, Sunsama, etc.), all do intake and reflection. **None closes the loop** from acting → did it work → revise the vision. That gap is where a *guide* beats an *archive*.

## Decision
Treat the closed reflection loop as LifeGuide's defensible center. Even though the full loop ships post-v1, the Mirror is designed from v1 to track **outcomes**, not just inputs, so the loop has data to stand on.

## Consequences
- v1 data model leaves room for goals/moves + outcomes.
- The daily ritual and the Coach are framed around "did today point at it?" not "log more."
- Time-binding (calendar) and the alignment engine become the v2 vehicles for the loop.

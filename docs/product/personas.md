# Personas

**Status:** 🟡 outline

## Primary — the lost young man
22–40, capable, ambitious-ish, but **directionless**. Aware something's off; no framework for figuring out what he wants or staying aligned with it. Walked a path that was set for him (school → job → money) and senses he never chose it. Lives on his phone, impatient with complexity, allergic to anything that feels like a chore, a lecture, or a productivity system. Wants a space that *gets* him and gently keeps him pointed somewhere — without nagging.

**Jobs:** get what's in my head out · figure out what I actually want · be reminded of it daily · have someone who knows me help me move.

## Anti-persona — the GTD shopper
Looking for a better to-do app / productivity system. LifeGuide is upstream of that; they'll bounce when they find no project view, deadlines, or task management. That's correct — don't design for them.
